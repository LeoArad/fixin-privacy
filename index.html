What is Fixin’?

Fixin’ is a social event coordination app that allows groups to plan gatherings, manage shared responsibilities, and track expenses. Whether you're organizing a BBQ, birthday, or game night, Fixin’ ensures everyone knows what to bring and how much they owe.
